About Me

I am Kritika from Ramnagar, Uttrakhand (near Jim Corbett).

I have been working as a freelance makeup artist since 2019. I have worked with more than 200 clients so far. I provide make up services for Bridal, Party, Pre wedding, Engagement, Sangeet, Reception, Fashion show, Haldi and Mehndi. I have strict work ethics. Providing client satisfaction along with using good products are always my topmost priority.
